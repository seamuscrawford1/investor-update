3.4 Expansion Guarantee

Both parties are committed to expanding the partnership beyond outbound sales. To align incentives and protect Versa's upfront technology investment:

Expansion Commitment: Claro shall use reasonable commercial endeavours to introduce at least one inbound customer care use case within 6 months of the Sales Pilot go-live date.

Pre-Payment Mechanism: If Claro has not introduced an inbound care use case by the end of month 6, Claro shall pay Versa a monthly pre-payment of USD $25,000, commencing in month 7 and continuing monthly until the earlier of:

  • (a) Claro introducing an inbound care use case;
  • (b) the parties entering into a broader commercial agreement; or
  • (c) termination of this agreement.

Credit mechanism: All pre-payments accumulate as a credit balance in favour of Claro. Upon commencement of live billable calls for inbound care, Versa shall apply the accumulated credit against invoices until the credit balance is fully exhausted.

This structure is modelled on Versa's existing enterprise agreements. It ensures both parties have skin in the game: Claro is incentivised to move quickly on care expansion (avoiding pre-payments), and Versa's investment is protected if expansion is delayed. The credit mechanism means Claro never "loses" the pre-payments — they are simply applied to future services.
